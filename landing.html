What it does

Everything.
One place.

Auto Detection
Automatically scans and imports your Steam, Epic Games, Minecraft, and Hytale libraries. Zero manual setup.
Custom Folders
Organize your library into collections. Right-click any game to add it instantly. Folders persist across sessions.
Add Any Game
Not in Steam or Epic? Add any .exe directly. Browse to it, name it, and it lives alongside your other games.
Minecraft Launchers
Detects 10+ launchers — Official, Lunar, Badlion, Prism, MultiMC, Modrinth, CurseForge, TLauncher, and Skyware Client.
Discord RPC
Rich presence in Discord. Shows "In the Launcher" while browsing and switches to the game name the moment you launch.
Music Player
Built-in ambient music player in the sidebar. Fades out on game launch, fades back in when the game closes.
